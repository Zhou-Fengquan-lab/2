课题组简介 About Our Lab

学术带头人周峰泉教授 Principal Investigator: Professor Fengquan Zhou

周峰泉教授，教育部CJ讲席学者，浙江大学求是讲席教授。于南京大学生化系获得本科与硕士学位，后赴美国纽约州立大学布法罗分校(SUNY at Buffalo)攻读解剖与细胞生物学专业，并获得博士学位。在美国北卡罗莱纳州教堂山分校(UNC-Chapel Hill)神经科学中心经过博士后培训，2005-2022年，在美国约翰斯-霍普金斯大学医学院(Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine)骨外科及神经科学系历任助理教授、副教授和终身正教授。 Professor Fengquan Zhou is a Ministry of Education "CJ Scholar" Chair Professor and a Qiushi Distinguished Chair Professor at Zhejiang University. He obtained his Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Biochemistry from Nanjing University. Subsequently, he pursued his Ph.D. in Anatomy and Cell Biology at the State University of New York at Buffalo (SUNY at Buffalo), USA. After completing postdoctoral training at the Neuroscience Center of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC-Chapel Hill), he served from 2005 to 2022 in the Department of Orthopaedic Surgery and Neuroscience at the Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, where he progressed through the ranks from Assistant Professor to Associate Professor and ultimately to Tenured Full Professor.

周峰泉教授2022年7月回国后担任浙江大学求是讲席教授，及浙江大学医学院附属邵逸夫医院神经再生转化中心负责人。长期致力于神经发育，神经损伤和修复相关的研究工作，在神经再生领域取得了多项创新成果。 Professor Fengquan Zhou returned to China in July 2022 and currently serves as a Qiushi Distinguished Chair Professor at Zhejiang University, as well as the Director of the Neural Regeneration and Transformation Center at Sir Run Run Shaw Hospital Affiliated with Zhejiang University School of Medicine. He has long been dedicated to research in neural development, nerve injury, and repair, and has achieved multiple groundbreaking findings in the field of neural regeneration.
