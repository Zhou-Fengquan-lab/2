课题组简介 About Our Lab

学术带头人周峰泉教授 Principal Investigator: Professor Fengquan Zhou

于南京大学生化系获得本科与硕士学位，然后前往美国纽约州立大学布法罗分校 (SUNY at Buffalo) 获得解剖与细胞生物学博士学位。在美国北卡罗莱纳州教堂山分校 (UNC-Chapel Hill) 神经科学中心经过博士后培训后，于2005-2022年在美国约翰斯-霍普金斯大学医学院 (Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine) 骨外科及神经科学系历任助理教授，副教授，和终身正教授。 Professor Zhou received his bachelor's and master's degrees from the Department of Biochemistry at Nanjing University, then obtained his Ph.D. in Anatomy and Cell Biology from the State University of New York at Buffalo. After postdoctoral training at the Neuroscience Center of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC-Chapel Hill), he served as Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, and tenured Full Professor in the Department of Orthopedic Surgery and Neuroscience at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine from 2005 to 2022.

周峰泉教授2022年7月回国后担任浙江大学求是讲席教授，及浙江大学医学院附属邵逸夫医院神经再生转化中心负责人。周教授长期致力于神经发育，神经损伤和修复相关的研究工作，尤其在神经再生的分子及细胞机制研究领域具有国际领先地位。 After returning to China in July 2022, Professor Zhou was appointed as Qiushi Chair Professor at Zhejiang University and Director of the Neural Regeneration and Translational Center at Sir Run Run Shaw Hospital, School of Medicine, Zhejiang University. Professor Zhou has long been dedicated to research in neural development, neural injury, and repair, and holds an internationally leading position in the field of molecular and cellular mechanisms of neural regeneration.

研究领域 Research Areas

中枢神经系统损伤、修复与再生 CNS Injury, Repair and Regeneration

中枢神经系统损伤，修复，与再生研究,如脑损伤，脊椎损伤，和视网膜损伤与疾病 （青光眼、黄斑病变及许多遗传性眼科疾病）。重点方向为细胞重编程和染色质重塑 Research on central nervous system injury, repair, and regeneration, including brain injury, spinal cord injury, and retinal injury and diseases (glaucoma, macular degeneration, and various hereditary eye diseases). Our focus is on cell reprogramming and chromatin remodeling.
