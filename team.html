课题组负责人 Principal Investigator

周峰泉 教授 / 博士生导师 Prof. Fengquan Zhou / Ph.D. Supervisor

浙江大学邵逸夫医院神经再生转化中心主任 Director of the Center for Translational Neuroregeneration at Sir Run Run Shaw Hospital, Zhejiang University

周峰泉教授于南京大学生化系获得本科与硕士学位，然后前往美国纽约州立大学布法罗分校获得解剖与细胞生物学博士学位。随后在约翰霍普金斯大学医学院完成博士后训练，并在该校担任助理教授十余年，2019年全职回国加入浙江大学。 Professor Fengquan Zhou received his bachelor's and master's degrees from the Department of Biochemistry at Nanjing University, and then obtained his Ph.D. in Anatomy and Cell Biology from the State University of New York at Buffalo. He completed his postdoctoral training at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, where he also served as an Assistant Professor for over a decade before returning to China in 2019 to join Zhejiang University.

周教授是国际知名的神经发育与再生领域专家，研究方向包括轴突生长与再生、神经元极性建立、细胞骨架动力学以及脊髓和视神经损伤后的再生修复策略。 Professor Zhou is an internationally recognized expert in the field of neural development and regeneration. His research focuses on axon growth and regeneration, neuronal polarity establishment, cytoskeletal dynamics, and regenerative strategies following spinal cord and optic nerve injuries.
