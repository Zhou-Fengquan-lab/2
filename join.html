博士后研究员 Postdoctoral Researcher

神经科学方向 Neuroscience

负责开展神经退行性疾病机制研究及新型治疗方法开发，具体研究方向包括但不限于： Responsible for conducting research on mechanisms of neurodegenerative diseases and developing novel therapeutic approaches. Research areas include but are not limited to:

  • 阿尔茨海默病和帕金森病的发病机制研究 Pathogenesis of Alzheimer's and Parkinson's diseases
  • 神经退行性疾病的早期诊断生物标志物开发 Development of early diagnostic biomarkers for neurodegenerative diseases
  • 基于基因编辑技术的神经疾病治疗策略 Gene editing-based therapeutic strategies for neurological disorders
要求： Requirements:
  • 神经科学、分子生物学或相关领域博士学位 PhD in neuroscience, molecular biology, or related fields
  • 熟练掌握分子生物学、细胞培养和动物模型相关技术 Proficient in molecular biology techniques, cell culture, and animal models
  • 有高质量SCI论文发表经历 Publication record in high-quality SCI journals
  • 良好的英语读写和沟通能力 Good English reading, writing, and communication skills

访问学者 Visiting Scholar

短期合作项目 Short-term Collaboration

课题组欢迎国内外高校、研究所的教师和研究人员申请访问学者项目，开展短期合作研究。 We welcome faculty and researchers from universities and research institutes worldwide to apply for visiting scholar positions for short-term collaborative research.

合作领域： Collaboration Areas:
  • 神经科学与神经疾病 Neuroscience and neurological disorders
  • 免疫学与自身免疫疾病 Immunology and autoimmune diseases
  • 基因编辑与基因治疗 Gene editing and gene therapy
  • 生物信息学与大数据分析 Bioinformatics and big data analysis
要求： Requirements:
  • 相关领域副教授及以上职称或具有同等研究能力 Associate professor or above in related fields, or equivalent research capability
  • 访问期间有明确的研究计划和目标 Clear research plan and objectives for the visiting period
  • 访问期限一般为3-12个月 Visiting duration typically 3-12 months
