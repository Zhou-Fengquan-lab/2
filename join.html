最新招聘职位 Open Positions

（一）浙江大学"百人计划"研究员 (1) ZJU "100 Talents Plan" Researcher

【申报条件】 [Requirements]
  1. 坚持立德树人，坚持正确政治方向，学风严谨，恪守师德行为规范和学术道德规范，具有良好的合作精神，对浙江大学文化和价值观高度认同； Adhere to moral education, maintain correct political direction, have rigorous academic style, abide by teacher ethics and academic norms, possess good collaborative spirit, and highly identify with Zhejiang University's culture and values;
  2. 科学研究取得突出业绩，具有发展潜力，属于国际科学前沿、主流及未来方向，符合学科发展需要，具有国际高水平大学助理教授或副教授相当学术水平，原则上能独立发展一个学术方向； Outstanding scientific research achievements with development potential, working in international scientific frontiers and future directions that meet disciplinary development needs, with academic level equivalent to assistant or associate professor at internationally renowned universities, capable of independently developing an academic direction;
  3. 教育背景和学术结构良好，相关研究成果得到国内外同行普遍认可，以第一作者或通讯作者发表高质量、高水平论文，或获得国际重要科技奖项，取得国际公认的科技成果。 Good educational background and academic structure, with research achievements widely recognized by domestic and international peers, publishing high-quality papers as first or corresponding author, or receiving important international scientific awards, achieving internationally recognized scientific results.
【岗位待遇】 [Benefits]
  1. 聘期内对"百人计划"研究员实行年薪制，一人一议； Annual salary system during the appointment period, negotiated individually;
  2. 提供具有竞争性的科研启动经费； Competitive research startup funding;
  3. 具有博士研究生招生资格，可优先安排博士生招生名额； Qualified to recruit doctoral students, with priority given to doctoral enrollment quotas;
  4. 按政策提供相应的办公、实验用房。 Provide corresponding office and laboratory space according to policies.
长期有效 Ongoing

（二）特聘（副）研究员 (2) Special-term (Associate) Researcher

【岗位职责】 [Responsibilities]

从事高水平基础和临床相关研究，开展原创性理论与实践问题研究或关键技术攻关，提高学科的国内外学术影响力。 Engage in high-level basic and clinical research, conduct original theoretical and practical research or tackle key technological challenges, enhance the academic influence of the discipline domestically and internationally.

【基本条件】 [Basic Requirements]
  1. 特聘研究员年龄不超过40周岁，特聘副研究员不超过37周岁； Special-term researcher age not exceeding 40, special-term associate researcher not exceeding 37;
  2. 博士学位，具有博士后研究工作经历； Doctoral degree with postdoctoral research experience;
  3. 有神经科学领域，生物信息学，或者表观遗传学研究经验者优先； Priority given to those with experience in neuroscience, bioinformatics, or epigenetics research;
  4. 以第一或通讯作者在相关领域发表至少1篇高水平文章； Published at least 1 high-level article as first or corresponding author in relevant fields;
  5. 有良好的师德师风、严谨求实的学风，有突出的学术发展潜力和合作精神。 Good teacher ethics, rigorous and practical academic style, outstanding academic development potential and collaborative spirit.
【岗位待遇】 [Benefits]
  1. 医院报备员额编制； Hospital registered position;
  2. 提供具有竞争性的薪酬和科研启动经费、一次性安家费； Competitive salary, research startup funding, and one-time relocation allowance;
  3. 支持申报研究生导师资格； Support for applying for graduate supervisor qualification;
  4. 特别优秀者一人一议。 Exceptional candidates will be negotiated individually.
长期有效 Ongoing

（三）助理研究员 (3) Assistant Researcher

【岗位职责】 [Responsibilities]
  1. 独立承担和开展课题研究； Independently undertake and conduct research projects;
  2. 独立申请项目并协助课题组申请项目； Independently apply for projects and assist the research group in project applications;
  3. 协助指导实验室其他成员开展科研工作。 Assist in guiding other laboratory members in scientific research.
【基本条件】 [Basic Requirements]
  1. 已取得（或即将取得）生物、医学相关科学博士学位； Obtained (or about to obtain) a doctoral degree in biology, medicine or related sciences;
  2. 具有创新性科研思维和较强的实验操作能力； Innovative scientific thinking and strong experimental skills;
  3. 具有较强的英文能力，能够独立撰写英文论文。 Strong English ability, capable of independently writing English papers.
【岗位待遇】 [Benefits]
  1. 医院报备员额编制，按照医院规定缴纳社会保险和住房公积金； Hospital registered position, social insurance and housing fund according to hospital regulations;
  2. 基础年薪25-30万元，根据科研成果发放额外奖励； Base annual salary of 250,000-300,000 RMB, with additional rewards based on research achievements;
  3. 具有完善的晋升渠道，如聘期内有优秀科研成果可根据医院政策申请相应职位。 Comprehensive promotion channels, excellent research results during the appointment period can lead to corresponding positions according to hospital policies.
长期有效 Ongoing

（四）博士后 (4) Postdoctoral Researcher

【岗位职责】 [Responsibilities]
  1. 独立承担和开展课题研究； Independently undertake and conduct research projects;
  2. 独立申请项目并协助课题组申请项目； Independently apply for projects and assist the research group in project applications;
  3. 协助指导实验室其他成员开展科研工作。 Assist in guiding other laboratory members in scientific research.
【基本条件】 [Basic Requirements]
  1. 近3年取得博士学位，或将于近期获得博士学位，欢迎二轮博士后入站； Doctoral degree obtained within the last 3 years, or about to obtain a doctoral degree; second-round postdocs are welcome;
  2. 具有良好的英文阅读、听力、口语表达及英文论文撰写能力； Good English reading, listening, speaking, and academic writing skills;
  3. 有较强的科研背景，以第一或通讯作者在相关领域发表高水平论文； Strong research background, published high-level papers as first or corresponding author in relevant fields;
  4. 神经生物学、细胞分子生物学、遗传学，生物信息学等相关领域； Neurobiology, cell and molecular biology, genetics, bioinformatics or related fields;
  5. 对科学研究具有较强的兴趣和热情，具有较强的独立工作能力，认真负责、积极主动、诚实守信、善于沟通，有团队合作精神。 Strong interest and passion for scientific research, strong independent working ability, responsible, proactive, honest, good communication skills, and team spirit.
【岗位待遇】 [Benefits]

博士后薪酬将根据个人科研工作能力和博士后有关规定从优发放。中心提供稳定的工作环境与一流的研究平台，协助申报博士后相关项目，根据兴趣与需求支持个人的职业发展。 Postdoctoral salary will be provided preferentially according to individual research ability and relevant regulations. The center provides a stable working environment and first-class research platform, assists in applying for postdoctoral projects, and supports personal career development based on interests and needs.

  1. 享受国家和浙江大学博士后待遇，年薪30万-40万（根据申报者个人业绩及表现进行核定）； Enjoy national and Zhejiang University postdoctoral benefits, annual salary of 300,000-400,000 RMB (determined based on individual performance);
  2. 博士后进站3个月内应完成开题，可申请确认助理研究员专业技术职务资格；人事关系进入学校的博士后人员在站时间满2年，可申报浙江大学专职研究副研究员职务任职资格； Postdocs should complete their research proposal within 3 months of entry and can apply for assistant researcher professional qualification; those with personnel relations in the university can apply for Zhejiang University full-time associate researcher qualification after 2 years;
  3. 博士后期间表现优秀，出站后可优先应聘我院员额编制岗位； Excellent performance during postdoctoral period can lead to priority for hospital registered positions after completion;
  4. 博士后在站期间，可以申请租住学校教师公寓； Postdocs can apply to rent school teacher apartments during their stay;
  5. 充分考虑博士后的发展和职业规划，大力支持博士后申报国家及省部级科研项目，提供参加国内外高水平学术会议或国际著名大学学习交流的机会。 Fully consider postdocs' development and career planning, strongly support postdocs in applying for national and provincial research projects, provide opportunities to participate in high-level academic conferences at home and abroad or study exchanges at internationally renowned universities.
长期有效 Ongoing

（五）科研助理(该岗位已招满，暂不开放) (5) Research Assistant (Position Closed)

【岗位职责】 [Responsibilities]

协助实验室科研人员开展课题，管理实验动物，协助行政助理完成实验室日常管理工作。对科研感兴趣或能力优秀者，能够独立承担部分科研项目及科研转化工作。 Assist laboratory researchers in conducting projects, manage experimental animals, assist administrative assistants in completing daily laboratory management tasks. Those interested in research or with excellent abilities can independently undertake some research projects and research translation work.

【基本条件】 [Basic Requirements]
  1. 生物相关专业本科及以上学历； Bachelor's degree or higher in biology-related fields;
  2. 责任心强，具有良好的沟通协调能力； Strong sense of responsibility, good communication and coordination skills;
  3. 具备生物化学、细胞生物学、分子生物学等基础知识； Basic knowledge of biochemistry, cell biology, molecular biology, etc.;
  4. 具备实验室小鼠实验操作经验者优先。 Priority given to those with laboratory mouse experimental experience.
【岗位待遇】 [Benefits]

根据邵逸夫医院相关规定以及申请人的工作能力而定，基础年薪11-13万元，在符合医院规定的条件下，根据科研成果发放额外奖励。 Based on Sir Run Run Shaw Hospital regulations and applicant's work ability, base annual salary of 110,000-130,000 RMB, with additional rewards based on research achievements under hospital regulations.

暂不开放 Not Available

（六）博士研究生 (6) PhD Student

课题组招收博士研究生，研究方向包括神经科学、生物信息学、表观遗传学等相关领域。 The research group recruits PhD students in fields including neuroscience, bioinformatics, epigenetics, and related areas.

欢迎对科学研究有浓厚兴趣，具有创新思维和扎实专业基础的优秀学子申请。 Excellent students with strong interest in scientific research, innovative thinking, and solid professional foundation are welcome to apply.

长期有效 Ongoing
